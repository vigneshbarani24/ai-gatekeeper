**Why This First**:
‚úÖ Biggest market (everyone has the problem)

## Why Start Consumer (Not Business)?

### Conventional Wisdom: "B2B is easier to monetize"
**True, but...**

### Counterarguments for Consumer-First:

1. **Bigger TAM**: 200M consumers vs. 5M businesses
2. **Faster Validation**: App Store launch in 2 months vs. 6-month B2B sales cycle
3. **Viral Growth**: Consumers share ("check out how I trolled a scammer")
4. **Data Flywheel**: More users = more scam call data = better detection
5. **Brand Awareness**: Consumer traction makes B2B sales easier later
6. **Exit Options**: Consumer apps get acquired (WhatsApp $19B, Truecaller unicorn)

### Why Consumer Apps Fail:
‚ùå High CAC (customer acquisition cost)
‚ùå Low retention (novelty wears off)
‚ùå Hard to monetize (expect everything free)

### How We Avoid These Pitfalls:
‚úÖ **Low CAC**: Organic (App Store SEO) + Viral (share scam call transcripts)
‚úÖ **High Retention**: Utility (people get spam calls daily, not a novelty)
‚úÖ **Willingness to Pay**: Proven (Truecaller Premium has 7M paying users)
